Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our rental process.

Do you provide certified operators regarding the rental?
Yes! We offer a team of NCCCO-certified operators available for hire with any equipment rental. You can also rent "bare" if you have your own qualified crew.
What is the minimum rental period?
We offer flexible rental terms starting from a single day. We also have discounted rates for weekly, monthly, and long-term project leasing.
Do you handle delivery and pickup?
Absolutely. We operate a dedicated fleet of lowboys and flatbeds to transport machinery directly to your job site. Delivery fees vary based on distance and equipment size.
What happens if the equipment breaks down?
We provide 24/7 mobile maintenance support. In the rare event of a breakdown, our technicians will be on-site immediately to repair or swap the machine to minimize your downtime.
Are fuel and insurance included?
Equipment is delivered with a full tank of fuel and should be returned full to avoid refueling charges. You must provide proof of "Hired In Plant" insurance, or you can purchase a damage waiver through us.
